Our Story

A Decade of Relentless Advocacy

PAAI was born from a simple observation: India's healthcare system, despite its advances, often left patients feeling voiceless and alone.

Our founders — patient advocates, former patients, and healthcare professionals — came together in 2012 to create a platform that would change that reality.

Today, with over 2,400 members and 180+ partner organizations, PAAI has become the backbone of India's patient advocacy movement.
